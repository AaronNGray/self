Kansas - a large, shared, flat space

A Morphic window shows just one portion of a large, two-dimensional world of morphs. The window can be panned around in this world, allowing different areas to be used for different activities.

Two tools are provided to pan the window: a radar view (prototype: radarView) shows a birds-eye view of the world around the current location, and has buttons to zoom the view in and out and move around the space. A world scroller (prototype: worldScrollerMorph) just has the navigation buttons.
