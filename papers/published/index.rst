A Self Bibliography
===================

Self has been the subject of a substantial number of academic papers over the years.

-------------
         
* `Programming as an Experience: The Inspiration for Self <published/programming-as-experience>`_ (1995), `Randall B. Smith <http://www.sun.com/research/people/randall.smith>`_ and David Ungar

* `Parents are Shared Parts: Inheritance and Encapsulation in Self <parents-shared-parts>`_ (1991), `Craig Chambers <http://www.cs.washington.edu/people/faculty/chambers.html>`_, David Ungar, Bay-Wei Chang, and `Urs Hölzle <http://www.cs.ucsb.edu/~urs>`_
